Message of the Day

Welcome to Excelsus! This is a Build and PvP server where creativity and combat collide. Respect others, play fair, and most importantly — have fun!

General Rules
  • 1. No prop killing.
    More Info
    Prop killing includes pushing, flinging, or crushing players using spawned props.
  • 2. No prop surfing.
  • 3. No prop blocking.
    More Info
    Blocking off areas of the map or trapping players with props is not allowed.
  • 4. No hacking or cheating of any kind.
  • 5. Respect all players.
    More Info
    Racism, harassment, or excessive toxicity will not be tolerated.
Building Rules
  • 1. Do not create laggy or crash-inducing builds.
  • 2. Keep builds appropriate for all players.
  • 3. Do not abuse building tools to gain unfair PvP advantages.
PvP Rules
  • 1. No spawn killing.
  • 2. Do not exploit map bugs or glitches.
  • 3. Play fair and avoid abusing mechanics.
