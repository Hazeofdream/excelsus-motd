General Server Rules
  • (1) Do not use any method or threaten to crash the server, even as a joke.
  • (2) Do not use third-party applications (Aka: Cheating) or use exploits.
  • (3) Do not DDoS or expose personal information of players. This includes threats of this nature.
  • (4) Do not abuse props, Such as: Proptrapping or Propkilling (Excluding Gravity Gun)
    Exemptions may be granted for this rule if it is handled under a different section, such as Wiremod Rules.
Wiremod Guidelines
  • Preface
    "Wiremod" is a reference to not just the addon, but expression2, simfphys and starfall as a whole.
  • Ultimate Summary
    "Do not use something you would not fight yourself." If you can't or wouldn't willingly fight something you made, it's either too strong, not fun, or downright mingey. Staff will ultimately decide if a contraption is an issue.
  • Notes
    Staff may ban contraptions even if not explicitly against rules. You may appeal to the "Wire / SF Committee". Committee members may exempt contraptions if they are creative or fun.
Rules
  • (1) All contraptions that resemble a mech or vehicle must be destructible. Public dupes must be damage-ready.
  • (2) Hitboxes must reasonably match visual appearance.
  • (3) Emplacements like artillery are exempt if stoppable (e.g., shooting the gunner).
  • (4) Anything that uses lock-ons is prohibited unless:
    (4a) The lockon informs the target (visual/audio) in a reasonable time.
    (4b) The weapon can be countered, e.g., dodging or blocking, unless evasiveness is the balance factor (like jet heatseekers).
